The **McLaren 720S** is a [sports car](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sports_car) designed and manufactured by British automobile manufacturer [McLaren Automotive] (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McLaren_Automotive). It is the second all-new car in the McLaren *Super Series*, replacing the [650S] (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/McLaren_650S) beginning in May 2017. The 720S was launched at the [Geneva Motor Show](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Geneva_Motor_Show#2017)  on 7 March 2017 and is built on a modified carbon monocoque, which is lighter and stiffer than the previous model, the 650S. Specifications[edit] The 720S is the first all-new car to be introduced by McLaren as a part of its new plan to launch 15 new cars into the market by 2022. Engine[edit] The 720S features McLaren's new M840T engine which is an evolution of the M838T used in the 650S.[9] It is a 3,994 cc (4.0 L; 243.7 cu in) twin-turbocharged V8 engine. [10][11] The engine has a rated power output of 720 PS (530 kW; 710 hp) at 7,500 rpm, giving the car its name; the maximum torque is 568 lb⋅ft (770 N⋅m) at 5,500 rpm.[12
